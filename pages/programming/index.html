Python

Python is the world's leading programming language for learning, creativity, and innovation. From AI and robotics to websites and data analysis, Python powers many of the technologies we use every day. Its clear, readable code makes it the perfect language for beginners and experts alike. It is also favoured by the OCR, so it makes sense to use what they use! It will aline closely to the mark schemes and while learning is the goal, a certificate with a 9 or an A* is what you can show people.
