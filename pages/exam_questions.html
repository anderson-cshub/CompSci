Assessment Objectives (AO1, AO2, AO3)

AO1 — Recall: Demonstrate knowledge of facts, definitions, and key concepts. This includes remembering syntax, terminology, and basic theory.

AO2 — Apply: Use your knowledge to solve problems. This includes writing algorithms, applying logic, and interpreting scenarios.

AO3 — Analyse: Break down problems, compare solutions, evaluate approaches, and justify decisions.

Understanding these objectives helps you target your revision and improve exam performance.

Exam Technique

  • Read every question twice before answering.
  • Underline command words such as describe, explain, evaluate.
  • Show working for algorithmic or logic questions.
  • Use bullet points for longer answers to stay structured.
  • Check your final answer for completeness and clarity.
